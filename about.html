Gathering Sounds
Collection About

Can humans communicate
solely through the use of sound?

The Gathering is a project on a sound archive platform designed to capture the essence of human experiences through the ambiance sound. This initiative aims to bridge the gap between analog and digital worlds, enriching people's encounters and fostering deeper connections.

Process

The project began with the collection of footage people gathering in various locations, starting from Downtown to College Hill in Providence, Rhode Island. This footage primarily captured the ambiance and atmosphere of these gatherings, with minimal conversation being captured. It was this initial collection that served as the catalyst for the “Gathering Sounds” project, which encourages the audience to participate by responding solely through sound.
